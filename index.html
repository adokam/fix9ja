📄 Terms & Conditions

Fix9ja Terms & Conditions — Last updated 2025

1. General

Fix9ja connects homeowners with verified service providers in Nigeria and acts as escrow agent for all transactions.

2. Wallet & Payments

All payments go through your Fix9ja wallet via Paystack. We do not store card details. Balances are non-transferable outside the app except via official withdrawal.

3. Escrow & Bookings

Payment is held in escrow until you confirm the job is complete. Fix9ja charges a 20% service fee per completed booking — providers receive 80%. Disputes must be raised within 24 hours.

4. Refund Policy

Full refund if provider no-shows. Dispute refunds are investigated within 48 hours and returned to your wallet within 24 hours of approval.

5. Service Providers

Providers must be approved by Fix9ja admin before listing. They are responsible for work quality. Repeated complaints lead to suspension or removal.

6. Referrals

Referral bonuses are credited after your referred friend completes their first transaction. Fraudulent referrals will be cancelled and accounts suspended.

7. Security

Keep your password secure. Fix9ja will never ask for it via WhatsApp or email. Report suspicious activity immediately to support.

8. Termination

Fix9ja may suspend or terminate accounts violating these terms without prior notice.
